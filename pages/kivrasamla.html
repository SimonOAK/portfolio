Brief

Kivra is Sweden's largest digital mailbox, and can be found in the phone of over 5 million Swedes. But even though the app is very well known, there is no emotional connection to the brand or knowledge of its many others services besides delivering your tax returns. It was time to strenghten Kivra's brand and give it a clearer position in the market.

Mission

As Creative Lead for the Kivra brand, me and the marketing team was tasked with a rehaul of the whole Kivra brand. We set out to create a brand platform and brand identity we could start speaking from that would allow us to have a more emotional connection to our users and senders, and make people realise there is more to Kivra than digital mail.

Creative platform

We launched the creative platform "En bättre plats för dina viktigheter" (A better place for your important stuff). A creative platform that will guide Kivra in both the continued development of the application (UX, UI, Research, Services) and its marketing. En bättre plats för dina viktigheter will be the springboard from which Kivra will not only speak, but prove that we are a better place for not only mail, but also collects all types of important documents that makes up our daily lives.

Have your collecting gone too far?

For the launch of "En bättre plats för dina viktigheter" we also created a campaign in which we showed Kivra's new playful tone and brand identity.

 

The campaign was aimed to position Kivra as a place for collecting more than just tax returns, but all types of important documents. From your own uploaded documents, to receipts, invoices, salary, contract and much more. Simply a place for people who takes their collecting very, very, very seriously.
