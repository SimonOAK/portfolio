Idea

To reduce food waste in Sweden, we helped Swedes learn to trust their senses instead of best-by dates by launching Old Milk – a perfume smelling like milk gone bad.

coop.se/old-milk

Insight and Concept

Over 60% of all food waste happens in people's homes. And in Sweden, 30% throw away edible food without smelling or tasting it. Coop, the country's most sustainable food chain, wanted to reduce the waste their costumers produce. But how? Well, because of labels such as best-by and best before, we have started to stop trusting our senses.

 

To change this, we launched Old Milk – a perfume that smelled like milk gone bad. The fragrance was made to help Swedes learn the real smell of bad milk and start thinking about their behaviour arond food waste.

Results

From the get-go, the fragrance was picked up by news media all over Sweden. Together with a national OOH campaign, perfume-events, influencer seeding kits and prints with scent-samples. There was no one in Sween who wheren't affected by the smell of Old Milk.
