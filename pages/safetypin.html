Student brief

Come up with an idea that can contribute to Trygghansa's commitment to safety around water with the purpose of reducing the number of drowning accidents in Sweden.

 

I created The Safety Pin. A waterproof sender you pin to your child's swimwear that transmits its location to a connected app.

Insight and Concept

Drowning is the leading cause of death among children aged 1-6 years. At the same time, the number of drowning accidents has been over the 100-mark for several consecutive years. What can Trygg Hansa do, an insurance company that constantly wants to improve safety around and in water?

 

As an insurance company, it's easy to only tell but not do anything, So I created the Safety Pin, a waterproof transmitter to pin to your swimwear. The transmitter is in the form of a safety pin with Bluetooth connection to a paired app. From the app you can see where the connected Safety Pin is located, and you get a warning when it loses connection, for example when it's submerged.
