Background

In Sweden (and in scandinavia), the amount of daylight is a huge topic for small talk. We love to talk about how we get less and less sun time during the autumn, and less and less darkness during the spring. Christmas? Halloween? No, the winter and summer solstice are the big events of the year.

Idea
