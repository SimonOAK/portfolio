About me

My name is Simon Kraft and I'm a multidisciplinary Art Director with experience leading and handling projects in advertising, design, UX, motion graphics and PR. I have worked for clients such as Google, Mini, Renault, Dove, Three, Arla Foods, UNICEF and Coca-Cola. I'm currently coming up with ideas and making them come to life at INGO Stockholm, Sweden.
