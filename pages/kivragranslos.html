Be Boundless with Kivra business

Background

What many people don't know is that Kivra is also available for businesses. How do we generate awareness for Kivra's Business offering among small and medium-sized enterprises? A service that allows businesses to receive all mail digitally, even from senders not connected to Kivra, as they can be scanned and delivered to Kivra.

Idea

With Kivra Business, you can eliminate all paper mail. You become free. You dare to cancel your office space, travel around the world, maybe finally get rid of that old printer that always malfunctions. You simply become more boundless (in swedish: "gränslös") as a business owner. Because you have (almost) no boundaries anymore. Using boundlessness as a creative springboard, we played with the idea of Kivra Business users becoming more limitless in their behaviour.
