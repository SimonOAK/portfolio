Idea

In collaboration between Coca-Cola and Star Wars we created the Feel the Force Machine, the world’s first interactive vending machine powered by the force of actual brain waves.

Brief and insight

For the release of “The Rise of Skywalker”, Coca-Cola designed 6 special edition Star Wars cans. But how do we help spark interest in the collection and encourage fans to collect them? We decided to make every Star Wars fan`s biggest dream come true: A way to use The Force in real life.

Campaign

By creating the Feel the Force Machine fans all over Europe got a chance to battle for a Coke with their minds in. The machine toured cinemas and Sci-Fi events before and during the release of the final Star Wars movie, where fans of every age was waiting in line to try the experience

How it works

By using EEG-headsets, the machine could analyze the electric activity in the user’s cerebral cortex, within the frequency range 4-40 HZ, the amplitude of the pulses in high frequencies (Gamma 13-39) and Beta (> 40 Hz).

 

This technology made it possible for the player whose brain activity measured the highest level of focus, to move the playing can to their side, leading to the machine dispensing one of the special edition cans for the winner.
