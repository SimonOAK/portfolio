Student brief

Come up with an idea for addressing and battling inequality between the genders.

Insight and Concept

Only 9% of articles on Wikipedia are written by women.

 

At the same time, women now outnumber men in attendance and graduation rates at universities the world over. These women write large number of papers that Wikipedia could use.

 

To make it easier for the uninitiated to add to Wikipedia I created a bot named Wicki. Wicki can automatically translate theses into articles for Wikipedia, which make it easy for women, or anyone else, who wants to contribute.

Results

Wicki was awarded in One Show with a Merit Award for Excellence in Advertising.

How Wicki works

Wicki is a simple formatting bot that makes theses (which are all written in a certain way) fit Wikipedia's format. So by using the bot on Wikipedia, Wicki implements your paper into the site with just the click of a button.

 

Wicki can also be used to scan old theses from university archives and add them to Wikipedia, making whole data banks of papers directly available as articles on the site.

Social Media

Wicki promotes itself and important topics that haven't been added or expanded upon on Wikipedia throught its own social media channels. Wicki can help students choose topics to write theses about and give tips on articles added to Wikipedia through Wicki.
