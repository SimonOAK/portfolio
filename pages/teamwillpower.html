Today, Swedish amateur athletes want to train, eat and dress like professionals. However, there is one thing that amateur athletes still have left to learn from the elite; mental training.

To show sports men and women the power of mental strength, Renault recruited a team of three athletes currently training for En Svensk Klassiker. After being trained by the professional coach Jonas Colting in a series of mental focus exercises, came the ultimate test - getting their minds connected to control and steer a Renault KADJAR together.

The experiment was picked up by both national and international outlets, resulting in a reach of 150 million in traditional media. We reached 4.5 million Swedes in social media, and the experiment got more than 1.6 million views.

But most importantly, we encouraged thousands of En Svensk Klassiker participants to train like professionals - focusing on mental strength, as much as the physical.
