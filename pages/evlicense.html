Idea

To bring awareness of Renault's work around electric vehicle, we created a proposition towards the swedish government for a specific EV-License. A license that would be heavily subsidized to make it easier to take, but only allow you to drive electric.

Visit the campaign site

Insight and Concept

In Sweden, taking a driver's license today isn't easy. Not only are there rigorous testing, you also have to pay for expensive driving lessons. Which has shown to exclude many who can't afford a license, but would need one the most.

 

Since Renault can't change the law, we created a proposition for a special driver's license that allows you to only drive electric: The EV-License. In return the license would be heavily subsidized, close to free and give you other percs as an electric driver.
