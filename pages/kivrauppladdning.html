Thank you for keeping Sweden together

Background

Kivra offers more than digital mail - one new service in Kivra is that you now have the ability to upload documents and important papers using your camera, so you can have even more viktigheter/essentials gathered in one place. However, this feature is not well-known. How can we make more people aware of the upload function among Kivra's current and potential users?

Idea

Paperclips, staples, and thumbtacks, items that have collected and held together our essentials for generations. They've done a good job, but now there's a better way: Uploading them to Kivra and keeping them safe and accessible wherever you are. With this insight, we launched the campaign "Thank you for keeping Sweden together" - a campaign under the concept of "A better place for your important stuff,‚Äù where we bid farewell to all the older items that have helped us gather essentials for so long, but now have a new competitor in the market: Kivra's upload service.
