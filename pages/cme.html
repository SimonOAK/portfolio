Idea

To market Renault family car MEGANE, we created an app named C-ME (Conversations Made Easy) that curates conversations during your car rides together, allowing everyone to talk about their own subjects and get the opinion of your family.

Try the app (only swedish or danish)

Insight and Concept

Today we can see an increase in time children spends in cars, and the same time people don't converse as they used to as the family dinner is becoming shorter and shorter. So how can Renault MEGANE, a car aimed at families, help them find time for each other?

 

We created C-ME, an app in your browser that curates conversational topics that the family members can add to the app and then talk about during car rides. With the curating system from the app everyone get time to talk and time to respond, allowing for more equal conversations.

Results

The app was picked up in national media outlets and was the most viewed campaign from Renault on social media.
