School project

As my graduation project in visual communication I created Moyo; an emotional training app in the form a digital creature that lives off your exercise. So when you stay healthy, Moyo stays healthy!

Insight and Concept

Research shows that fitness applications do not have the desired effect many want to believe. We buy and download these apps that measures our calorie expenditure, number of kilometers we run and how high our pulse is, yet we stop using them after a while.

 

Moyo is an emotional fitness app for smart watches that instead of displaying statistics and figures shows you a digital creature with a full emotional spectrum. Moyo feeds on your exercise, which means that you have to train to keep Moyo, but also yourself, in good health.
